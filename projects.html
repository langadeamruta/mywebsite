Shwas Tree Plantation and Sustenance Project

Shahapur & Jakatwadi are two villages where dumping yard of Satara Municipal Authority is situated. It makes the life of villagers so difficult. RC Satara and its members have initiated project of 'Shwas' where we have planted 123 trees in these villages which will help to purify the air and it will be a step towards creating green environment. Native Tree (such as Mango, Vad, Pipal, Neem, Palas, Jambhul) saplings were planted and will get monitored for next months. RC Satara will be working towards the sustenance of these trees. It is ensured that the trees are watered on regular basis and are growing in a good manner.
